How to Join

To maintain maximum confidentiality and security, we are no longer collecting information through this website.

If you are ready to sign your union authorization card or have questions about the process, please reach out through one of these secure channels:

  • Step 1: Talk to a trusted coworker who is already involved in the organizing committee.
  • Step 2: Email us at info@wehotempsunited.org from your personal email address.
  • Step 3: Text or call us at 415-949-0048.

⚠️ Security Reminder: Never use your weho.org email or work-issued devices to discuss union business. Always use personal accounts and devices.

All inquiries are confidential and protected by California's Meyers-Milias-Brown Act.
