We Are West Hollywood Temporary Employees United

Lifeguards and aquatics aides organizing for fair working conditions, job security, and a voice on the job with AFSCME.

Standing Together for Better Jobs

As temporary lifeguards and aquatics aides at the West Hollywood Aquatic Center, we deserve job security, fair treatment, and a real voice in our workplace. We're organizing with AFSCME to win these improvements.

Support is growing every day! Workers across all shifts and titles—aides, temporary lifeguard 1s, and temporary lifeguard 2s—are joining together. Talk to your coworkers about what we're building.
